About Us

Philosophy

Located in the heart of Jaipur city - Raja Park, Wearhouse is a one stop shopping destination for garments for the entire family.

At Wearhouse, we believe that success lies not in just meeting demands but above all, in innovating relentlessly. As a result, we've learnt to grow along with our customers. Setting trends and keeping in touch with the latest in local and international fashion.

Needless to say, we satisfy the strictest quality checks and the most discerning of customers,alike. The store is truly created out of pure love and great passion.
Commitment to quality - is the ultimate motto that we follow.

The Range

At any given point the store offers 10,000+ unique garments under one roof. Tall claim? But it's true. The offering includes t-shirts,shirts,jeans,pyjamas and pants for kids as well as adults.

Winterwear includes a wide variety of cardigans, long-coats and stoles for women. Jackets, sweaters and hoodies are also available for men as well as kids.

The sizes available at our store go upto 4XL. This ensures no one is left behind in following the latest fashion trends. Also, the prices for the plus sizes are similar to the regular ones.

Introducing Infantwear

The store has introduced a complete new section for children aged from 0-2 years. This section includes t-shirts,party-wear,pants and a whole lot more for the little ones. All the garments offered are made from extremely soft and lightweight fabric.
